# PitchCrush AI

## Product Name
PitchCrush AI

## Tagline
Decode what people really mean.

## Core Idea

AI-powered communication intelligence platform.

Users paste a message/chat/email and AI analyzes:

- Surface meaning
- Hidden intent
- Interest score
- Power dynamic
- Emotional tone
- Red flags
- Recommended reply
- Confidence score

## Goal

Build a real SaaS-feeling AI product, not a toy chatbot.

## Product Positioning

PitchCrush AI is not a chatbot.

It is a decision-support AI for communication.

## Target Users

- job seekers
- freelancers
- founders
- salespeople
- dating users
- professionals
- negotiators

## Core Promise

Understand what people truly mean before you reply.
